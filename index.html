Aspire, Believe, Achieve!

Tailored learning for children and adults

I am a fully qualified teacher with a huge experience in providing quality learning opportunities for individuals of all ages. Having taught for many years in different schools across the country as well as an adult learning centre I am adept at providing engaging lessons for all abilities that promote progress, confidence and self-esteem. For the last 4 years I have been providing private 1:1 tutoring in a variety of subjects. Much of my tuition has been requested by clients for an extended period as well as for different children in the same family. Tuition takes place in my own home to enable me to make the best use of a wide range of resources as well as outdoor learning opportunities for younger pupils.

The aim of my lessons is to promote learning and progression by building confidence through success and encouraging a ‘can do’ approach to learning. I plan tailored sessions specific to the needs of the individual which are interactive, enjoyable and fun.

I love to teach and have had much success with the individuals with whom I work. If you are looking for an enthusiastic teacher who will do their very best to help you or your child achieve their goals then please contact me.
